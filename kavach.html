Project Kavach

An Ultra-Low-Cost, Solar-Powered IoT Alert System for a secure, connected, and autonomous India.

üí° The Problem

Securing remote assets in India is challenging due to high costs, and lack of reliable power and internet.

üéØ The Solution

A decentralized, "fire-and-forget" alert system that is affordable, self-sufficient, and works anywhere with a mobile signal.

üîë Key Feature

End-to-end AES encryption ensures all captured data is completely secure from interception during transmission.
